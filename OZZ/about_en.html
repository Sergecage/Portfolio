History of OZZ club

OZZ club was founded in the summer of 2012.



During it's lifetime, the club changes several places.



A huge number of groups from all over the world performed on the club scene.



The following bands have played on our stage: 7Б | ЭПИДЕМИЯ | ПОРНОФИЛЬМЫ | МАСТЕР | STIGMATA | ПЛАН ЛОМОНОСОВА | КИРПИЧИ | МАРА | CATHARSIS | WILDWAYS | FACE | СЕРГЕЙ ТАБАЧНИКОВ И NOBODY.ONE | ППР | ГОД ЗМЕИ | BIG BABY TAPE | FPG | АФФИНАЖ | АНИМАЦИЯ | MEMORAIN | СЕРГЕЙ ЛЕТОВ | DISTEMPER | ЧЕРНЫЙ ОБЕЛИСК | БРИГАДНЫЙ ПОДРЯД | БАХЫТ КОМПОТ | PURGEN | МАВРИН | ГРОТ ,,, many others



The Club often hosts events with the participation of foreign guests, our guests were representatives from countries such as: Greece, France, Czech Republic, Sweden, Germany, Egypt, the Ukraine, Belarus, USA, Barcelona, England, Argentina, Brazil and others.
